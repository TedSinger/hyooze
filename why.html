Basic Ergonomic Colors

Here is a palette of colors in columns of equal brightness, with the maximum range of chroma:

Each of these four images shows colors arranged in columns by brightness, and placed vertically by hue. The result looks haphazard because of empty sections and because the hues do not align to any grid. Each image has the darker colors on a white background and the lighter colors on a black background



What for?

Start your application design in greyscale and ensure your application is legible. Then you can freely switch any grey to another color in the same column - because the brightnesses are the same, the contrast ratios and legibility will not be compromised.
